ENT501 · New Venture Finance
15 credits · Prof. Julia Daley

Module Description: This module provides a rigorous foundation in the financial mechanics of early-stage ventures. You'll master valuation, term sheets, cap tables, and fundraising strategies – essential tools for both founders and investors.

Weekly Breakdown

Week 1
Valuation Fundamentals
  • Venture Deals Ch 1-3
  • Case: "Uber's Seed Round"
  • Excel: Pre/post-money calculator
Week 2
Term Sheets: Economic Clauses
  • Venture Deals Ch 4-5
  • Case: "The Theranos Term Sheet"
  • Template: Term sheet comparison matrix
Week 3
Term Sheets: Control Clauses
  • Venture Deals Ch 6-7
  • Case: "Facebook's Early Rounds"
  • Simulation: Negotiation role-play
Week 4
Cap Tables & Dilution
  • Case: "Seed to Series B"
  • Excel: Multi-round cap table
  • Exercise: Down round modelling
Week 5
Convertible Notes & SAFEs
  • YC SAFE documentation
  • Case: "Note vs. Priced Round"
  • Modelling: Valuation caps & discounts
Week 6
Fundraising Process
  • Pitch deck examples
  • Guest: VC partner
  • Assignment: Investment memo

Assessment: Individual investment memo (40%) + term sheet negotiation simulation (30%) + cap table modelling exercise (30%)
