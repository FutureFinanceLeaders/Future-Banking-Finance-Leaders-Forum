2. ACADEMIC RATIONALE

The combination of a BSc in Finance & Investment and an MBA in Entrepreneurship & Innovation creates a powerful interdisciplinary skill set:

  • Finance Foundation: The BSc provides rigorous training in valuation, financial modelling, capital markets, and investment analysis.
  • Entrepreneurial Application: The MBA applies these financial skills to venture creation, startup funding, and innovation management.
  • Synergistic Learning: Finance modules directly reinforce MBA modules (e.g., Principles of Finance → New Venture Finance; Corporate Finance → Venture Capital & Private Equity).
  • Career Integration: The dual qualification prepares the student for roles at the intersection of finance and entrepreneurship, including venture capital, private equity, startup CFO, and corporate innovation.

This pathway embodies the Nolan University mission: "Where Company Builders, Investors, and Innovators Are Educated."
