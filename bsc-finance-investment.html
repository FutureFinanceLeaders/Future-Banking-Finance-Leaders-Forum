📋 Overview

Shape the future of global finance. Nolan’s BSc in Finance & Investment blends rigorous quantitative training with real‑world investment practice. You will master financial modelling, portfolio management, and ethical decision‑making – taught by research leaders and industry practitioners.

The programme is designed in close collaboration with the CFA Institute and prepares you for roles in investment banking, asset management, and financial analysis. From day one you engage with Bloomberg terminals, trading simulations, and spring week preparation.

🔎 Detailed courses (core modules)

FN1002 – Principles of Finance 15 credits
Time value of money, valuation, CAPM, market efficiency. Includes Bloomberg Market Concepts.
FN2005 – Equity & Fixed Income Analysis 20 credits
DCF, relative valuation, bond pricing, duration, credit risk. Real company cases.
FN2010 – Financial Modelling 15 credits
Build integrated 3‑statement models, DCF, LBO, and scenario analysis in Excel + Python intro.
FN3012 – Advanced Portfolio Management 20 credits
Mean‑variance optimisation, factor models, performance attribution, and trading strategies.
FN3020 – Applied Investment Project 30 credits
Capstone: manage a real £100k virtual portfolio, pitch to industry panel.
