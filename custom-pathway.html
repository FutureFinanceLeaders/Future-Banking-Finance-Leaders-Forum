💼 After Year 1: You Have an MBA

With your MBA certificate awarded after Year 1, you can pursue MBA-level roles while completing your BSc:

  • Venture Capital: Analyst/Associate roles
  • Private Equity: Associate positions
  • Investment Banking: MBA associate programmes
  • Startup: Founder or early employee with MBA credibility
  • Corporate Finance: Finance Manager, Analyst
  • Consulting: Associate Consultant

You may work part-time or full-time while completing your BSc, or focus on studies and start your career immediately after BSc completion.
