M. Lewis Capital Partners

Scholarships: Legacy-impact tuition scholarships for MBA and Bachelor’s students. Conditional on GPA and leadership/impact projects.

Loans: Flexible founder-style loans to cover tuition or living costs. Repayment can be deferred or income-linked.

AfriLeap Capital

Grants: Non-repayable grants for student innovation, prototypes, research, or entrepreneurial projects.

Loans: Flexible loans for tuition or living costs, tied to project milestones or revenue from ventures.

Nolan University Merit Scholarships

Term-based academic merit scholarships automatically applied for eligible students, reducing overall tuition and loan dependency.
