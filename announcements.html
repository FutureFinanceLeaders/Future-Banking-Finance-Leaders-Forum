Nolan University Announces Strategic Funding Partnership with M. Lewis Capital Partners and AfriLeap Capital

Nolan University is pleased to announce a strategic partnership with M. Lewis Capital Partners and AfriLeap Capital to introduce a comprehensive, term-based student funding system for its Bachelor’s and Master’s degree programmes.

This partnership establishes an integrated model in which funding is embedded directly into the academic journey, enabling students to access not only education, but also structured financial and developmental support throughout their studies.

  • Nolan University: Provides merit-based scholarships to support academic excellence
  • M. Lewis Capital Partners: Offers Legacy-Impact Scholarships and flexible student funding to support leadership development and long-term impact
  • AfriLeap Capital: Provides innovation grants and structured funding for academic projects, research, and applied learning initiatives

All funding mechanisms are aligned with Nolan University’s academic framework and are applied within approved degree programmes. This ensures that students are supported holistically—academically, financially, and developmentally—while maintaining a clear focus on outcomes within their field of study.

This partnership reflects Nolan University’s commitment to advancing a new model of higher education—one that integrates learning, funding, and real-world application into a unified student experience.
