Welcome to the Hybrid Funding System

Nolan University, in partnership with M. Lewis Capital Partners and AfriLeap Capital, offers a term-based hybrid funding system for undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This system allows students to:

  • Receive scholarships for tuition and academic excellence
  • Access grants for innovation, entrepreneurship, and project work
  • Obtain flexible, founder-style loans with deferred or income-linked repayment

The program is optional and designed to support students who want to combine academic achievement with entrepreneurial and impact-driven projects.
