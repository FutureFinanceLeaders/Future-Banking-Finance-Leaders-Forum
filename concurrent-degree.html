📅 TERM 1 · FINANCIAL TOOLKIT · April – July 2026

Entrepreneurial Finance & Fundraising
8 credits · 12 weeks
  • Master cap tables, term sheets, valuation, convertible notes
  • Build investor-ready financial models
  • Fundraising mechanics and investor negotiations
    • Synergizes with: BSc Corporate Finance
    Entrepreneurial Mindset & Opportunity
    8 credits · 12 weeks
  • Opportunity recognition and customer discovery
  • Effectuation and creative problem-solving
  • Conduct 20+ customer interviews
    • Synergizes with: BSc Behavioural Finance
    Financial Accounting for Entrepreneurs
    7 credits · 12 weeks
  • Understand financial statements and accounting principles
  • Make data-driven financial decisions
  • Build financial literacy for early-stage ventures
    • Synergizes with: BSc Financial Accounting
    Corporate Finance Fundamentals
    7 credits · 12 weeks
  • Capital budgeting and cost of capital
  • Valuation basics and financing decisions
  • Tailored for high-growth companies
    • Synergizes with: BSc Corporate Finance
    📊 Term 1 Total: 30 credits · Typical weekly workload: 35-40 hours

    📅 TERM 2 · THE INVESTOR'S DEEP DIVES · August – December 2026

    Venture Capital & Private Equity
    8 credits · 12 weeks
  • LBO modelling and fund economics
  • Carry waterfalls, deal sourcing, due diligence
  • LP-GP dynamics — build a full fund model
    • Synergizes with: BSc Alternative Investments
    Growth Strategy & Scaling Ventures
    8 credits · 12 weeks
  • Product-market fit and growth metrics
  • Scaling strategies and unit economics
  • Go-to-market execution for high-growth companies
    • Synergizes with: BSc Strategic Management
    Financial Modeling for Startups
    7 credits · 12 weeks
  • Three-statement models and scenario analysis
  • Sensitivity tables and investor-ready projections
  • Advanced Excel for venture finance
    • Synergizes with: BSc Financial Modelling
    Deal Structuring & Negotiation
    7 credits · 12 weeks
  • Term sheet anatomy and valuation methodologies
  • Negotiation tactics for founders and investors
  • Simulated deal negotiations
    • Synergizes with: BSc Investment Banking
    📊 Term 2 Total: 30 credits · Typical weekly workload: 35-40 hours

    📅 TERM 3 · CAPSTONE & SCALE · January – April 2027

    Venture Practicum (Capstone)
    15 credits · 12 weeks
  • Launch a real venture or complete an investment thesis
  • Full financial projections and unit economics
  • Pitch deck and Demo Day presentation to investors
    • Synergizes with: BSc Applied Investment Project
    Advanced Valuation & Exit Strategies
    10 credits · 12 weeks
  • DCF, comparable analysis, and LBO models
  • M&A strategies, IPOs, and exit planning
  • Maximize value for founders and investors
    • Synergizes with: BSc Mergers & Acquisitions
    Global Immersion (Elective)
    5 credits · 1 week
  • One-week immersion in Silicon Valley, London, or Nairobi
  • Meet founders, VCs, and innovation leaders
  • Ecosystem mapping and networking
    • Synergizes with: BSc Global Finance
    📊 Term 3 Total: 30 credits · Capstone intensity: 15-20 hours/week + valuation module
