💼 Career Outcomes

Graduates of the MBA in Entrepreneurship & Finance are equipped to lead at the intersection of building and funding ventures:

  • Founder / CEO: Launch and scale your own venture with deep financial expertise
  • Venture Capital Associate: Source, evaluate, and invest in high-growth startups
  • Startup CFO / Head of Finance: Lead financial strategy and fundraising for high-growth companies
  • Growth Equity Analyst: Invest in scaling companies with proven business models
  • Corporate Innovation Lead: Drive innovation and new venture development within organizations
  • Angel Investor / Venture Partner: Invest in and mentor early-stage founders

Average Salary Range: $80,000 – $180,000 + equity/carry depending on role and experience.
