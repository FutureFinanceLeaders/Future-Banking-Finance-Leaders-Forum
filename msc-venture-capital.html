💰 1-year MSc · Venture Capital & Private Equity

📋 Overview · Master the deal

For future leaders of the private capital markets. The MSc in Venture Capital & Private Equity is a highly intensive programme designed to equip you with the technical, analytical, and deal-making skills required to excel in VC, PE, and growth equity.

You'll master LBO modelling, term sheet negotiation, fund structuring, due diligence, and portfolio management – learning directly from practising investors and entrepreneurs. The curriculum is co-designed with leading firms including Balderton, Index Ventures, and Permira.
