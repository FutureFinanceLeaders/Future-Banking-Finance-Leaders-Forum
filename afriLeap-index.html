Our Mission

The firm focuses on enabling the workforce of tomorrow by deploying innovative financing mechanisms such as income-share agreements, tuition financing, and seed capital for early-stage ventures.

By investing in human capital and entrepreneurship, AfriLeap Capital supports students in accessing education, enables graduates to launch businesses, and helps SMEs scale operations—driving sustainable job creation and economic transformation across Sub-Saharan Africa.

What We Support

🎓 Education Financing

Income-share agreements and tuition financing helping students access world-class education.

🚀 Graduate Venture Funding

Seed capital and venture support enabling graduates to launch innovative startups.

🏢 SME Growth Capital

Investment and financing for SMEs creating jobs and scaling businesses across Africa.
