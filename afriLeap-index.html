AfriLeap Capital is a specialized impact investment firm and capital access platform dedicated to empowering students, graduates, and SMEs to build businesses and create jobs.

The firm focuses on enabling the workforce of tomorrow by deploying innovative financing mechanisms such as income-share agreements, tuition financing, and seed capital for early-stage ventures.

Our Financial Products & Services

AfriLeap Capital is a specialized impact investment firm building a ladder of opportunity for Africa's job creators. We use innovative financial structures—including blended finance, guarantees, and patient capital to support the riskiest stages of entrepreneurship where traditional banks fear to tread.

As our clients grow, we grow with them, offering larger and more competitive financial products to fuel their expansion. We don't just provide capital—we provide a financial home for life.

Our Core Financing Architecture

We deploy Blended Finance using development grants, first-loss capital, and risk guarantees to unlock funding for students, graduates, and SMEs who would otherwise be excluded from the financial system.

Early Opportunity Capital

Designed for students and early-stage founders beginning their journey.

  • Income Share Agreements (ISAs)
    Students pay nothing upfront. Repayment begins only after employment through a small percentage of income.
  • Grant-First Loans
    A portion of the funding is structured as a grant (funded by partners) to support training and education costs for students at Nolan University.

Growth Capital

For graduates and young SMEs beginning to generate revenue and requiring capital to scale operations.

  • Growth Loans ($5k – $50k)
    Flexible capital designed for businesses with early revenue and basic financial records.
  • Asset Financing
    Funding for equipment, machinery, or technology where the asset itself acts as partial collateral.

Expansion & Institutional Capital

For established SMEs with strong cash flow, financial records, and a proven track record of job creation.

  • Expansion Loans ($100k+)
    Long-term financing for scaling operations and entering new markets.
  • Trade Finance
    Short-term funding that enables businesses to fulfill large supply contracts.
  • Equity Investments
    Minority growth investments in high-potential companies.

Our ultimate mission is simple: to provide every type of capital a job creator needs—from the classroom to a thriving SME.

Our Capital Architecture

AfriLeap Capital structures innovative blended finance vehicles that combine philanthropic capital, development finance, and commercial investment to unlock funding for Africa’s next generation of entrepreneurs and job creators.

Philanthropic Grants

Provided by foundations and development partners to support training, capacity building, and risk absorption.

↓

First-Loss Capital

Impact investors absorb initial risk which allows the fund to support entrepreneurs that traditional banks would reject.

↓

Commercial Capital

Banks, institutional investors, and funds provide scalable capital once risk is reduced through the blended finance structure.

↓

Students, Graduates & SMEs

The capital ultimately flows to Africa’s job creators — funding education, launching new ventures, and scaling businesses that employ the workforce of tomorrow.

By combining multiple forms of capital into one structure, AfriLeap Capital reduces risk for investors while expanding opportunity for entrepreneurs. This model enables sustainable job creation and inclusive economic growth across Africa.
